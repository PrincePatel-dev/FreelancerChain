# FreelancerChain ⛓️

**FreelancerChain** is a trustless, decentralized reputation platform built on the Avalanche Blockchain. It empowers freelancers to build an unfakeable, permanent on-chain portfolio while ensuring clients get the work they paid for through smart contract escrow.

Unlike traditional platforms, FreelancerChain eliminates the middleman, reduces fees to 0%, and ensures that every review and rating is backed by a real, verifiable transaction on the blockchain.

## 🚀 Features

*   **Trustless Reputation:** Ratings and job history are stored permanently on the Avalanche blockchain.
*   **Smart Escrow:** Client funds are locked in a smart contract and only released when the job is completed and rated.
*   **0% Fees:** Direct peer-to-peer payments with no platform cuts.
*   **Verifiable Profiles:** Anyone can verify a freelancer's history by identifying their wallet address.
